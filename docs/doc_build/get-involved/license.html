    #What This Means

    FreedomDescription

    #Key Points

    • No license fees — Free forever. No per-user, per-invoice, or per-domain charges
    • No vendor lock-in — Your data is in your database on your server
    • Open formats — JSON, CSV, PDF, DOCX, XLSX exports
    • Standard SQL — Queryable, exportable, migratable data
    • Copyleft — If you distribute modified versions, they must also be GPLv3
