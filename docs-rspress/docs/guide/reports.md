# Reports

Generate financial and operational reports to track your business performance.

## Available Reports

### Sales Reports
- **Sales by Period**: Total sales grouped by day, week, month, or year
- **Sales by Customer**: Revenue breakdown per customer
- **Sales Total**: Aggregate sales with filtering options

### Debtor Reports
- **Debtors by Amount**: Outstanding balances ranked by amount owed
- **Debtors by Aging**: Unpaid invoices grouped by age (30, 60, 90+ days)
- **Debtors Owing by Customer**: Detailed breakdown per customer

### Profit Reports
- **Invoice Profit**: Revenue minus product costs per invoice
- **Biller Total**: Revenue grouped by biller
- **Biller by Customer**: Cross-reference of billers and customers

### Product Reports
- **Products Sold Total**: Quantity and revenue per product
- **Products Sold by Customer**: Which customers buy which products

## Using Reports

1. Go to **Reports** in the navigation
2. Select a report from the list
3. Set date ranges and filters as needed
4. Reports with charts will render automatically using your data

## Charts

Most reports include interactive charts powered by ApexCharts:
- **Bar charts** for comparisons
- **Line charts** for trends over time
- **Pie/Donut charts** for distribution
- Hover over chart elements for detailed tooltips

## Exporting Reports

Report data can be exported:
- View the report table on screen
- Use browser print for a PDF/paper copy
- Copy-paste data into spreadsheet applications for further analysis
